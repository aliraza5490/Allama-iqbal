Sir Allama Muhammad Iqbal

The poet of the East

Here's a time line of Iqbal's life:

  • 1877 Born on 9 November, 1877
  • 1897 He received degree of Bachelor of arts in philosophy, English literature, Arabic
  • 1899 He received Master of arts degree from Government College University
  • 1903 Travelled to England
  • 1906 Obtained a Bachelor of arts in England
  • 1907 Moved to Germany in 1907
  • 1908 Earned a Doctor of Philosophy degree from the Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich
  • 1908 In 1908 returned from England
  • 1926 In November , contested for a seat in Punjab Legislative Assembly
  • 1930 In presidential address on 29 Dec, outlined a vision of an independent state for Muslim-majority provinces
  • 1930 Iqbal was elected president of the Muslim League
  • 1932 He reiterated the ideas of his 1932 address, and, during the third Round Table Conference
  • 1933 He was invited to Afghanistan to join the meetings regarding the establishment of Kabul University
  • 1938 Died on April 21, 1938
